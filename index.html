About Us

Centre for Rural and Entrepreneurship Development (CRED) offers a comprehensive range of programs and initiatives aimed at promoting rural development, entrepreneurship and skill development. These include various training programs to enhance entrepreneurial skills, fostering entrepreneurship in rural communities, supporting innovative ideas, conducting impact studies of government schemes related to rural development, providing skill development training, organizing Training of Trainers (ToT) and Training of Assessors (ToA) programs.

CRED collaborates with prominent skill development organizations such as the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), Tamil Nadu Skill Development Corporation (TNSDC), and other skilling agencies. This partnership aims to enhance and strengthen skill development initiatives, ensuring a more comprehensive and effective approach towards developing the skills of individuals in rural areas. By forging with these agencies, the center leverages its expertise, resources, and networks to create impactful skill development opportunities and empower individuals with relevant skills for better economic prospects and livelihoods.
